Kawaii Fighters

Player 1 (Blue): A/D to move, W to jump, S to crouch, Q to punch (combo!), E to kick (strong!), R to block, F to shoot beam

Player 2 (Red): Left/Right arrows to move, Up arrow to jump, Down arrow to crouch, , (comma) to punch (combo!), . (period) to kick (strong!), / to block, Right Shift to shoot beam
